Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This privacy policy explains how Byte Me PC / Smartphone Repairs North East ("we", "us", "our") handles any personal information collected through this website (bytemepc.com).

Who we are

Byte Me PC / Smartphone Repairs North East is a local electronics repair business based in Craghead, County Durham, DH9 6EB. You can contact us at repairs@bytemepc.com or 07807 174740.

What information we collect

When you submit the contact form on our website, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number and/or email address
  • A description of how we can help you

We do not collect any other personal data and we do not use cookies or tracking on this website.

How we use your information

The information you submit via the contact form is used solely to respond to your enquiry. It is sent directly to our email inbox and is not stored in any database or third-party system beyond standard email.

Legal basis for processing

We process your data on the basis of legitimate interest — specifically, to respond to your request for information or a repair quote. You have the option to contact us by phone if you prefer not to submit your details online.

How long we keep your data

Contact form submissions are received as emails and retained only as long as necessary to handle your enquiry. We do not maintain a separate database of customer enquiries.

Third-party services

This website uses the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare — provides hosting infrastructure and security. Cloudflare may process your IP address as part of routing traffic. See Cloudflare's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — used for website typography. Loading fonts from Google's servers may transmit your IP address to Google. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Google Maps — the address link on this site opens Google Maps in a new tab. No data is sent to Google unless you click the link. See Google's privacy policy.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict how we process your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (Information Commissioner's Office)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at repairs@bytemepc.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
